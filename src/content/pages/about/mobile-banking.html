To access the 1st Choice Mobile Banking App, follow these simple steps:

  • You must already be registered for 1st Choice Credit Union's Online Banking. If you are not yet registered, please go online to www.1stchoicecu.org and sign up.
  • Next, using your smartphone or mobile device, access the App Store (Apple Store for iPhones, iPads and Google Play for Android).
  • Search for the terms: 1st Choice CU and follow the download instructions.
  • Once the app has been downloaded to your device, you can open it and follow the directions on the screen. Your log in credentials will be the same as those you have set up for your Online Banking.
