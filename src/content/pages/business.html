Business

We offer Business Savings and Business Checking Accounts.

A total of $150 is required to establish membership; $50 to open the Business Savings (membership requirement), and $100 to open the Business Checking.

There are two types of identities:

  • Sole Proprietor – This is one person that is doing business. The social security number will be used to identify the identity of the sole proprietor.
  • Corporation, LLC, INC, Trade Organizations – These organizations will have a Tax Identification Number. This number is used when checking the identity of the business.
  • Basic Business Checking
  • Prime Business Checking
  • Business License, Articles of Incorporation, Limited Liability Certificate, Internal
  • Revenue Tax Payer ID Letter
  • Ordering Checks:
    • Checks can say DBA (Doing Business As)
    • Check packages range from $52.34 and up
  • Overdraft Protection is not available on Business Account
  • $5 fee for mailed statements, fee is waived if enrolled in E-Statement
